Test Instructions

  • It is Highly Recommended that Students should use a Laptop or a Desktop to take the VINit for the Best Experience.
  • Ensure that no Other Application/File/Folder/Website/Tab is open at the Time of VINit.
  • Ensure that your Device is Equipped with a Functioning Camera and Microphone.
  • ⁠Cross-Check your Question Paper to ensure it is appropriate for your chosen class and opted stream.
  • Verify that you have a Stable and High-Speed Internet Connection for the VINit.
  • If the candidate is using a mobile device to take the test, they should use the Chrome web browser on both iOS and Android. The Android operating system must be version 11 or higher, and the device should be connected to Wi-Fi for optimal connectivity.
  • ⁠Student should Use Google Chrome to Attempt the VINit. Please Enable Notifications on your Browser to get Pop-up Notifications.
  • Please note that your activities are being monitored, and any use of unfair means may negatively affect your overall score.
  • ⁠Students Must Login 15 minutes prior to the VINit Time to avoid last moment rush.
  • ⁠Duration of Test is 2:30 hrs First Shift will Start at 10:00 am and ends at 12:30 pm. In Second shift the Test will Start at 3:00 pm and ends at 5:30 pm.
  • Students Should Start the Test within 30 mins from the Start Time of the Respective VINit.
  • Your Username for the VINit is your Roll Number followed by@vmc.com (e.g., 1234567@vmc.com), and your Password is your First Name in Lower Case (e.g., First Name is ABC, Password will be abc).
  • ⁠To Move to Next Question Student Must Click Save and Next Button. To End the Test Student Should Click Submit Button. If the Next Question is not visible on your screen, please wait for a while. Don’t Click on Submit Button.
  • ⁠It is Recommended to Review Sample Papers beforehand to gain Clarity on the Syllabus and Marking Scheme.
  • There will be Negative Marking for Wrong Answers in VINit.
  • ⁠In Case of any Difficulties or Have any Queries During the VINit, please Contact your Preferred VMC Centre or Contact at 9818795768.
  • Maintain Integrity During the VINit by Refraining from Using any Unfair Means, Such as Cell Phones, Log Tables, Smart Watches, Books, or Making any Movements that may be Detected by the Camera, Switching Between Tabs.
  • ⁠Adhering to these Instructions will Help Ensure a Fair and Smooth VINit Examination Process
